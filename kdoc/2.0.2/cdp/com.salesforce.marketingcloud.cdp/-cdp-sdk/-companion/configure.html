Initializes the SDK with the provided CdpConfig. Once complete, the InitializationListener will be notified.
Any subsequent calls to this method will be ignored as long as the values provided in CdpConfig are the same.
Note: Anyone that calls requestSdk before or during the initialization will be notified before the InitializationListener will be notified.
